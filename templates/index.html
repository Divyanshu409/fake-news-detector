Under the hood

How Fake News Detector works

A four-stage pipeline from raw text to verdict.

01
Tokenization

Your article is split into subword tokens by DistilBERT's vocabulary — up to 512 at once.

02
Transformer encoding

6 attention layers build deep contextual representations — word meaning relative to everything around it.

03
Classification

A fine-tuned linear layer maps the [CLS] embedding to FAKE / REAL probabilities via softmax.

04
Signal extraction

High-frequency content words are surfaced, showing which terms most influenced the verdict.
